QUICKSOMA

ABOUT US

We discovered that their is a problem, where pregnant teenage girls
who drop out of school are unable to continue their education while on maternity.
We also found out that when they return to school they have forgotten
what they learned .We decided to come up with Quicksoma platform which is an
interactive learning channel that provides revision questions for them to
continue learning while on Maternity.

HOW IT WORKS

QuickSoma is an offline learning platform that uses a USSD code
with the goal of assisting pregnant teenage students while on maternity.
They will use feature phones to help them access and tackle questions
that will help them revise and stay on top of their
academics in a variety of subjects, such as mathematics.
